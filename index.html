Why it matters to all of us
🌾

Food chains collapse

Each extinction ripples through the food web, destroying the balance that feeds us all.

💧

Clean water at risk

Wetland animals like otters and beavers keep our rivers and water sources healthy.

🌿

Medicines disappear

Over 50% of medicines come from nature. Extinction means cures we haven't found yet are lost forever.
